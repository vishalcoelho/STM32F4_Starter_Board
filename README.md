# STM32F4_Starter_Board
KiCAD 9 project for the STM32F405RGT6 starter board

---

I have been following [Phil's Lab Tutorial](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=C7-8nUU6e3E) on designing your first PCB in KiCad and using JLCPCB as the assembly house. I did the entire project in KiCAD 9, which differs slightly from the video, and ended up taking notes :memo: and screenshots :camera: along the way, which I put into a four part blog:

1. :triangular_ruler: [First STM32 PCB Design in KiCad and JLCPCB Assembly: Part I - Schematic Layout](https://vishalcoelho.github.io/blog/kicad/pcb-design/kicad-stm32-usb-pcb-design-jlcpcb-assy-part-1/)
2. :world_map: [First STM32 PCB Design in KiCad and JLCPCB Assembly: Part II - Board Layout](https://vishalcoelho.github.io/blog/kicad/pcb-design/kicad-stm32-usb-pcb-design-jlcpcb-assy-part-2/)
3. :traffic_light: [First STM32 PCB Design in KiCad and JLCPCB Assembly: Part III - Routing](https://vishalcoelho.github.io/blog/kicad/pcb-design/kicad-stm32-usb-pcb-design-jlcpcb-assy-part-3/)
4. :factory: [First STM32 PCB Design in KiCad and JLCPCB Assembly: Part IV - Manufacturing and Assembly](https://vishalcoelho.github.io/blog/kicad/pcb-design/kicad-stm32-usb-pcb-design-jlcpcb-assy-part-4/)

> :pray: Apologies if the blog doesn't read like a normal tutorial, they really were notes I took for later reference.
